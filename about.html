DATA SOURCE


The HR-Attrition dataset is a common dataset used in data science and machine learning applications to study and analyze employee attrition and its related factors. The dataset typically includes information about employees, such as their age, gender, job role, years of experience, education level, job satisfaction, performance ratings, salary, and other relevant factors.

The specific data sources for the HR-Attrition dataset can vary, but it may be obtained from various HR management systems or employee databases. Some organizations may release anonymized or aggregated versions of their own HR-Attrition dataset for research and analysis purposes. Additionally, some third-party data providers may offer HR-Attrition datasets that are compiled from multiple sources and can be used for analysis.

It's important to note that any use of HR-Attrition dataset must comply with data privacy laws and ethical guidelines. Researchers and data analysts should ensure that the data is anonymized or de-identified to protect employee privacy and confidentiality.
